Breakfast

Start the day off strong!

Our catering has delivered the highest quality catering experience tailored to each client’s specific needs – all of which equals an immense value that is waiting to for you! Our recipe is easy – high quality, down-to-earth, homemade catering for every menu item we offer – made fresh and prepared from scratch. It’s simple – Just. Good. Food. Serving the entire greater Pittsburgh region and the Tri-State area of Pittsburgh, East Ohio, and West Virginia, we will travel to meet your needs. Send the catering inquiry form to get your party started!




Joe and June Harold founded the restaurant at its current location in Hopewell. They began a restaurant success story that centers around fair prices, generous portions, and a homestyle menu that has something to please everyone. Homemade daily The soup selections and daily sandwich and dinner specials are offered for variety and allow Harold's to try out new items before adding an item to the regular menu. Most items, like the fish sandwich, have been featured on the menu since the first day of operations in in 1962.
