About Us


Tradition since 1962

Joe and June Harold founded the restaurant at its current location in Hopewell. They began a restaurant success story that centers around fair prices, generous portions, and a homestyle menu that has something to please everyone. Homemade daily The soup selections and daily sandwich and dinner specials are offered for variety and allow Harold's to try out new items before adding an item to the regular menu. Most items, like the fish sandwich, have been featured on the menu since the first day of operations in in 1962.
